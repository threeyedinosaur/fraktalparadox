SEIKILOZ

The Seikiloz Epitaph is the oldest known written tune, dating from more than 2000 years ago. This ancient treasure inspires the name of this app that will give you, so to say it, musician 'super-powers'. Perhaps you want to experiment with new tools for your new compositions, or maybe you are looking to create the music that will give your game that grim vibe you are chasing. Whatever you are looking to achieve with music composition, this tool will assist you to create music without any need of musical notation, harmony theory or anything else that our beloved musicians do to delight our hearing. Of course, if you are a musician or want to beyond basic, just enable our advanced options!

Main features:

    Create from short sound effects to large classical lke compositions.
  • Simple and easy to use interface that can be transformed in an airplane-like dashboard with advanced tools so you can neatly fly trough your musical inspiration.
  • Export the result in the format you want to and, if it's your desire, share the result with the world.

We work with you from the development of your idea till the practical implementation of the developed product. All of our apps are easily auditable by anyone thanks to it's open source nature.
