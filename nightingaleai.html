NIGHTINGALE AI

Technology has been historically excluyent over our most experienced partners: the elderly people. This technology is aimed at providing an all in one solution that turns android devices in 24/7 nurses ready to serve in everything a smartphone can do for an old person. From using Google services to answer their questions to keep track and create reminders of the drugs taken, pending doctor appointmens, that next birthday or assisting in recording and sending trough WhatsApp or Telegram a video message of that loved one.

Main features:

  • Ultra-easy to use app designed to avoid misuse by it's users (like closing it or negatively changing the configuration) and equally easy configuration from the care giver.
  • AI everywhere. We have designed the app to squeeze all the power AI has to give to our wise old people.
  • Whitelabel option so health services providers can have a tailor-made solution.

We work with you from the development of your idea till the practical implementation of the developed product. All of our apps are easily auditable by anyone thanks to it's open source nature.
